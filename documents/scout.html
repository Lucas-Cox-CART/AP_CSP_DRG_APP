Meet the Scout!

The Scout is the most selfish of the dwarves as while the other three have equipment to help the entire team traverse the caverns of Hoxxes IV, the scout has a grappling hook only for himself. This also makes him the speediest dwarf. Along with his grappling hook, he has a flaregun which can stick flares to the walls and ceiling of the cave to make for better visibility. Besides that, he has cryo grenades, an extrmeley powerful M1 Garand, a laser rifle, a double barrel shotgun, and an incredibly high-utility crossbow.

Meet the Driller!

The Driller is the most useful class for getting his team safely to the drop-pod and allows for his team to get through the mission quicker by driller down big walls of dirt. He also happens to be the class most responsible for killing his entire team by throwing C4 at them. The Driller has the most effective swarm-clearing arsenal of any of the dwarves bearing a flamethrower, cyrothrower, and a sludgethrower; all of which have very powerful upgrades.

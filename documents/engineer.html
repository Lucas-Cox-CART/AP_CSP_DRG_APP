Deep Rock Galactic

Meet the Engineer!

The Engineer is the most passive class of all the dwarves, they can set up turrets to automatically kill swarms of glyphids. They also have a platform gun which is supremely useful for breaking falls and giving ground for Scouts to stand on while they extract ores. Their arsenal consists of a long-range shotgun, a breech-loaded grenade launcher, a 'smart' rifle, and possible one of the best weapons in the game: The Breech Cutter.
